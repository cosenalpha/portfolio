Stadia Hunt
Team: Martina Accetta, Diletta Catti, Sarah Cosentino, Claudia Dal Santo, Giada German√≤, Giulia Schena

Service design

Sep 2021 / Jan 2022

Stadia Hunt is a service design project that deals with the issue of loneliness and the lack of human contact, especially during the fourth industrial revolution. The target of the service is mainly gamers between the ages of 14 to 25.

It relies on the gaming platform Google Stadia, which allows people to play different videogames through a single account. The service works as a communication channel between the gaming routine and the outer world by offering a reward system for going outside and engaging in the activities that the service itself selected.

The Stadia Hunt mobile app offers an AR section, through which gamers can scan the so-called LockBoxes, which contain Doubloons, rewards that can be used in any game on Google Stadia, but also discounts to local activities.
In fact, any local business can buy or rent a LockBox close to the shop to put discounts in it, in order to attract more clients. Gamers are invited to unlock the Boxes with other people since doing so increases their luck, therefore giving better prices.
